Summary

I'm a Postdoctoral fellow in AI.MED lab at University of Alabama at Birmingham, advised by Dr. Jake Chen. My past research focuses on biological data mining, systems biology, network biology, artificial intelligence, visual analytics and translational informatics to improve human health. I’ve constructed several webservers and applications for functional genomics downstream analysis and drug repositioning. My current research involves characterizing driving genes and underlying molecular mechanisms using multi-omics data and single-cell data.
